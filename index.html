我在南京的时间

The pictures below are some of my favoirte from my time in the city.

In fall 2015, I moved to the city of Nanjing, China to work as an English teacher. I had never visited Asia before and spoke no Chinese but that didn't stop me from quickly becoming enammored with the city I would call my home for nearly four years. This webpage is dedicated to the city of Nanjing.

The Qinhuai River was once an important artery that moved good and supplies through the city of Nanjing.

Today the Qinhuai river largely serves as a lovely landmark and recreation area but some traces of industry and commerece remain. Lets take a look at one of my favorite spots: The open air spice market.

During my time as a teacher, I had students as young as three and others nearing eighty. Learning a new language opens door no matter your age whether for commincating with others or computers ;)
